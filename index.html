About

Poonam is a Certified Counsellor, Counselling Psychologist, and a Facilitator dedicated to empowering individuals in their transformation journey.
She possesses a Master’s degree in Counselling Psychology and has completed a one-year certification programme in counselling skills. Additionally, she holds certification in counselling children and adolescents. In her previous avatar, leveraging her Master's degree in Computer Applications, she served as a software engineer for a prestigious company.
Poonam possesses a comprehensive background that includes individual counselling, facilitation programs, and workshops on EQ, all rooted in holistic mental well-being. She believes in a holistic and harmonious integration of her skills to empower her clients.
Currently, Poonam serves as a counsellor and facilitator at Prerana Academy, Counselling and Training Centre in Bengaluru. She is also associated with the NGO SAMVAD and maintains her own private practice. Her passion for helping others thrive is the driving force behind her work.

Approach to Counselling

Counselling provides a secure and confidential environment where you are encouraged to openly discuss a wide range of topics, including your emotions, life circumstances, experiences, challenges, issues, and dilemmas. It serves as a platform for exploration, understanding, the identification of concerns, and their subsequent resolution.
Poonam refrains from providing advice or solutions. Instead, counselling is an empowering journey in which you will be guided towards developing the capacity to independently resolve your own problems.
Her role as a counsellor, she will assist in fostering increased self-awareness, support you in effectively addressing your challenges, and collaborate with you on personal growth and relationship enhancement as you strive to achieve your objectives. The counselling process will proceed at a pace that aligns with your comfort and readiness as her client. Therefore, the duration of the counselling process will be influenced by factors such as the complexity of the issues, the specific goals established, your availability for sessions, and your level of readiness to engage in the process.
She will keep records of the sessions, and these records will be kept confidential.
